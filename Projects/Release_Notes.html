Release Notes for FP-SNS-SMARTAG2 Projects

Copyright © 2023 STMicroelectronics

Purpose

The FP-SNS-SMARTAG2 Projects provide applications to read the ambient light, motion and environmental sensor data on your IoT node by the means of an NFC enabled reader, such as a mobile phone or tablet.

Supported Devices and Boards

  • STEVAL-SMARTAG2 (SmarTag2) evaluation board that contains the following components:
    • MEMS sensor devices (LPS22DF, STTS22H, LIS2DUX12, H3LIS331DL, LSM6DSO32X)
    • ambient light sensors(VD6283TX)
    • dynamic NFC tag (ST25DV64KC)
